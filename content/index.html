Welcome to my Website!

Who am I?

My Name is Cam :) I'm a PhD Candidate from Australia. I work on laser systems and funny glass tubes. I also really likes coffee, beer, history, and plenty of other things.

What Do I Do?

I use lasers to talk to satellites. Check out this blogpost if you want to know what that means!

What is this website?

This website is my personal blog, and home for my digital identity.

The posts are a mix of personal and professional, and represent my personal thoughts and noth that of any employer or institution.

I rarely manage to stick to a single topic in my blog. Common themes revolve around interesting science (mainly physics), personal opionions and documenting my work (both professional and personal).

I'm a big fan of "learning in public" so if I figure something out that I could not find a tutorial for, I will typically write one up here. See more in `/Colophon`
