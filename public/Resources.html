Resources that I put together!

  • Reddit RSS Generator A simple tool to generate RSS feed URLs for any subreddit. Perfect for following subreddits in your favorite RSS reader without needing a Reddit account.
  • ChiScraper! The most useful tool I have ever made, and I legitimately use it every day. ChiScraper pulls articles from ArXiv, and ranks them based on its relevance to your interests. It is a great way to keep up to date with the latest research in your field.
  • LoFi Radio! A terminal based LoFi Radio that I made in Python. It is super simple and minimalist to stay distraction free!

    Other Tools That I like:

    Kill The Newsletter! I read my RSS feed every day. It is where I get my news, check the ArXive and read the blogs of other people I follow. I have the problem that some people have replaced the blog with an email newsletter. I DO NOT WANT MORE emails in my inbox. this is where kill the newletter comes in. They give me an email alias to sign up for newsletters, and it converts each email it recieves into an RSS feed for me to view at my lesuire. Plus, it is free and Open Source. Thanks Leonardo!

    Just A QR Code: Does what it says on the tin. It no ads or BS. Even better, you can save the webpage offline and it still works.
